### Suggested Clarification to Enrollment Scenario Description
Amy noted that the current wording does not clearly communicate that the scenario involves:
- a current-year enrollment, and
- a separate switch for the upcoming year.

Suggested wording addition:

> “During OEP, you may assist beneficiaries who are new to Medicare PDP/MA-PD with an enrollment for this year (see the New to Medicare PDP/MA-PD section above for qualifications) and choose to switch to a different PDP/MA-PD with coverage beginning in the upcoming year.”

#### Reason
- Reinforces that two enrollment periods/years are involved.
- Helps distinguish the scenario from standard New to Medicare enrollments.

---

### Suggested UX Improvement
Suggestion:
- Limit the “First Month of Coverage” dropdown to:
  - November
  - December

for the New and Switching calculator.

#### Reason
- Reinforces the enrollment timing specific to this scenario.
- May help reduce user mistakes or invalid scenario use.

#### Status
- Implemented May 2026.
- Dropdown options were updated to limit selection to November and December.
