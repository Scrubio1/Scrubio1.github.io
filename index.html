New to Medicare PDP/MA-PD (this year) and Switching PDP/MA-PD (next year)

Original PDP/MA-PD Cost Calculator

Prescriptions on a 30-Day Refill Schedule

During OEP, you may assist beneficiaries who are new to Medicare PDP/MA-PD (see the New to Medicare PDP/MA-PD section above for qualifications) and choose to switch to a different PDP/MA-PD with coverage beginning in the upcoming year. Be careful to select the correct year when comparing costs in the MPF.

This enrollment scenario is unique because it requires three sets of Plan Details:

  • New to Medicare PDP/MA-PD Plan Details – Plan Details for the initial PDP/MA-PD enrollment for the current year.
  • Current PDP/MA-PD Plan Details for Next Year – Plan Details for the PDP/MA-PD initially enrolled in but reflecting costs for the upcoming year.
  • Switching PDP/MA-PD Plan Details – Plan Details for the PDP/MA-PD the beneficiary is switching to for the upcoming year.
